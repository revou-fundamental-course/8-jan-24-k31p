What is Kalkulator Matematik Persegi?

Kalkulator Matematik Persegi is a website that helps you compute the area or perimeter of a square by the given square side length

How to use

You just need to input your square's side length and what operation you want to use (area or perimeter)

How to find the area and the perimeter of the square

  1. To find the square area, multiply the square side by the square's own side ( side ✕ side )
  2. To find the square perimeter, multiply the square side by 4
